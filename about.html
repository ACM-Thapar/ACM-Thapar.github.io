The ACM Student Chapter

Thapar ACM Student Chapter

Thapar ACM Student Chapter is a technical society of TIET, Patiala. Under the guidance of one of the most premier organizations in the computing world, ACM, we strive towards a single goal i.e. improving the coding culture at Thapar. With some of the most brilliant minds of Thapar working as the backbone of the society, we are leaving no stones unturned. Organizing classes and workshop for everyone in the college, as we firmly believe that “Learning knows no boundaries.” Not only this, we also encourage participation in different events amongst our family, who have then gone to great heights like cracking ICPC, winning hackathons and the list just goes on. Our members have also been selected for internships in some amazing companies like Microsoft, Goldman Sachs, J.P. Morgan, etc. In the end, we are just a bunch of peeps who like to code, have fun and maybe, change the world?.

Why ACM

We believe that every member should benefit from events, workshops and interactions.

Academic Program

From Data Structures and Algo to Ethical Hacking, we provide quality and efficient teaching which develops critical thinking and problem solving.

Skill Development

Companies are looking for Skilled Engineers, by conducting numerous workshops and Guest Talk we expose the members to nearly every field in Computer Science.

Student Mentor Environment

Frequent classes on crucial topics are the peak importance, they provide the fundamentals of good programmer. Hence we have Student-Mentor evnvironment for efficient learning

Opportunities

Thapar ACM conducts numerous events and provides ocean of opportunies such as Hackathons, Event Organization, and many more.
